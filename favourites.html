    General

    If it has sci-fi as a main theme, I am interested. I like the majority of sci-fi based things simply because of how large in scale the worlds can get and how massive the history of the worlds are.

    I do admit to a love of horror, the most obvious style being the horror of the unfathomable (aka H.P Lovecraft) and the darker takes on the world at large.
